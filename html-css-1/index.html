Things to do in Austin Texas

Austin is a great city for outdoor enthusiasts. It's home to many parks and lakes which are perfect for those who love to hike and swim. Here are a few places to cool off when you visit Austin.

  • Paddleboarding
  • Barton Springs
  • River Float

Go Stand Up Paddleboarding

Renting stand up paddle boards is a fun way to see the Austin cityscape. There are plenty of rental places close to Ladybird Lake, which is the water way that runs along Downtown.

Visit Barton Springs

Barton Springs is a great spot to cool off. The spring is bordered by grassy areas perfect for laying down your towel and catching some rays. Whilst swimming you can enjoy the cityscape view of Downtown Austin.

Go on a Riverfloat

Austin has plenty of rivers that are perfect for a riverfloat. San Marcos River is just one of them. Make sure you check the rules as some rivers have banned plastic containers, and hefty fines are handed out to those who don't abide. Riverfloating is amazing if you want to be surrounded by 56,935 other people blasting all sorts of crappy music. It can be really peaceful.
